Welcome to MarketReplay

Trade stocks using real historical market data. You pick the time period — then navigate day by day, making buy and sell decisions just like a real trader.

Your goal: grow your portfolio before the simulation ends. Don't go broke!

📅

The Game Loop

Each click of Next Day → advances one trading day. Charts update, prices change, and your portfolio shifts in real time.

Use ⏩ Skip Days to jump multiple days at once — great for holding through a trend. You'll receive a full report of everything that happened.

💰

Buying & Selling

BUY — Purchase shares. Profit when the price rises above your average cost.

SELL — Close a long position. Profit or loss is realized and added to your cash balance.

Use the Max button to automatically fill the maximum affordable quantity.

📉

Short Selling

SHORT — Bet that a stock will fall. You borrow shares and sell them, keeping collateral. Profit when you buy back cheaper.

COVER — Close a short position. If the price dropped, you profit. If it rose, you lose.

⚠️ Shorts require full cash collateral equal to the position value.

🧩

Charts & Portfolio

The left panel shows your open positions. Click any position to instantly load its chart and fill the trade form.

The centre chart shows candlestick price history — capped at the current in-game date. No peeking ahead!

The watchlist bar below the chart lets you track multiple tickers side by side.

🏁

Winning & Losing

The simulation ends when you reach the end date you selected, or your total portfolio value hits $0.

At the end you get a full report: grade, best & worst trades, win rate, and total return.
